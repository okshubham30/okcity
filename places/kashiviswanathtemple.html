Kashi Vishwanath Temple & BHU

Complete Overview by OK Varanasi

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Located on the western bank of the Ganga River in Varanasi. The temple is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas and attracts millions of devotees every year.


Highlights: Jyotirlinga, Ganga Aarti, Vishwanath Corridor, Spiritual Importance, Ancient History.

Banaras Hindu University (BHU)

BHU is one of India's largest and most prestigious universities. Founded by Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya in 1916, it offers education in science, engineering, medicine, arts, agriculture and more.


Highlights: IIT BHU, Bharat Kala Bhavan, Vishwanath Temple, Huge Campus, Research Excellence.
